Frequently Asked Questions

When is the family reunion?

Friday, July 16 through Sunday, July 18, 2027.

Where is the host hotel?

Hilton Garden Inn Atlanta East/Stonecrest, 7890 Mall Ring Road, Lithonia, Georgia.

Who should pick up the registration bags?

The family member who made the reservation or payment should pick up all bags. They will be held under that person’s name.

What should we wear for the family photo?

Please wear your Family Reunion T-shirt if possible. Everyone is welcome to wear whatever makes them comfortable.

Is Sunday mandatory?

No. Sunday’s fellowship service and brunch are optional.

Can children attend?

Yes. Children are welcome at the family-friendly reunion events.
